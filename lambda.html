Core

Cost

AWS Lambda's pay-per-use pricing model offers a cost-effective solution for businesses.

With Lambda, you only pay for the compute time consumed by your functions, without incurring charges for idle resources.

This cost-efficient approach allows businesses to optimize their spending while benefiting from the scalability and flexibility offered by serverless computing.

Scalablility

One of the key advantages of AWS Lambda is its inherent scalability.

Lambda automatically scales to accommodate varying workloads, seamlessly handling fluctuations in traffic without any manual intervention required.

Whether you're experiencing a surge in demand or facing periods of low activity, Lambda dynamically adjusts resources to match your application's needs, ensuring optimal performance and responsiveness at all times.

Effeciency

AWS Lambda shines in its efficiency, offering a streamlined deployment process that maximizes productivity.

Leveraging integrations like GitHub, developers can seamlessly automate deployment workflows, ensuring rapid and reliable updates to Lambda functions.

Additionally, Lambda integrates seamlessly with various AWS services, allowing for efficient data processing, storage, and management within the AWS ecosystem.

Lambda Use Cases

Khumla

Param Solutions developed a rental asset management tool integrating with Sage Accounting Software, utilizing React for the front end, .Net6, SQL Server, and AWS S3, the solution offers features such as asset registration, Sage integration, rental management, reporting, and more.

Khumla chose AWS lambda as their core computing infrastructure due to its scalability, reliability, and extensive set of services, allowing for the creation of a robust and flexible multi-tenant application while ensuring high availability and security.

Param Solutions developed a comprehensive online learning platform for Khumla powered by a microservice mesh that utilizes RabbitMQ & Redis, Mongo DB, and AWS services for scalability and reliability. The solution incorporates features such as behavioral-based learning, gamification elements, quizzes, assessments, and multi-tenancy support to enhance learner engagement and performance.

Khumla integrates AWS Lambda as their primary computing platform, drawn to its scalability, reliability, and comprehensive service offerings. This choice enables the development of a resilient and adaptable multi-tenant application, bolstered by seamless integration with other services like RabbitMQ. The result is a solution that ensures high availability and security while empowering Khumla to meet diverse business needs efficiently.
