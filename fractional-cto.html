What is a fractional CTO?

A Fractional CTO is a part-time tech leader for startups. They guide technology strategies, make critical decisions, and contribute to building software, matching full-time expertise without the full-time cost. Benefits of a Fractional CTO:

Expertise on demand:
Get top-tier tech leadership when you need it.
Cost-effective:
Fractional services fit neatly into a lean startup budget.
Flexibility: 
Scale tech guidance with your business growth.
Focused execution:
Turn ambitious tech goals into tangible software.
Risk reduction:
Make informed decisions with professional tech insights.
Networking
Fractional CTOs can connect you with the people you need to know.
